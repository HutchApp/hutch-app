Save any web page with one click using the browser extension for Firefox or Chrome. Every saved article gets an AI-generated TL;DR summary. Use those summaries to decide what deserves your full attention.

## How it works

1. **Install the extension** for Firefox or Chrome.
2. **Click to save** any article you find.
3. **A summary appears** in your reading list.

## What the AI does

It generates a 2-3 sentence TL;DR for every saved article. The summaries highlight the most important points. Powered by DeepSeek and included in your subscription. No extra cost, no API keys.

## What the AI does not do

It does not replace reading. It helps you choose *what* to read.
